Security Architecture and Design is one of 10 domains comprising the Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) Common Body of Knowledge (CBK). In this CISSP certification prep course from LearnKey, expert instructor Michael Solomon discusses Bell-LaPadula, Clark-Wilson and other security models, as well as common security flaws. At the conclusion of this course, you will be familiar with the issues and concepts of the Security Architecture & Design domain required to pass the CISSP exam.
