In this course, you will learn the advanced skills needed to configure and maintain a highly available and scalable virtual infrastructure. The course is based on VMware vSphere ESXi 5.0 and VMware vCenter Server 5.1. You will prepare for the VMware Certified Advanced Professional - Datacenter Administration [V5] certification (VCAP-DCA). Completion of this course also satisfies the prerequisite for the VMware Certified Professional - Datacenter Virtualization (VCP-DV) certification.
