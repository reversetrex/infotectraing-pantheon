If you are looking to start a career in business or finance, having a 
working knowledge of Microsoft's Excel 2013 is a must. Our new and 
exciting course taught by Michael Meskers will teach you how to reveal 
insights hidden in your data with the help of new features such as Flash 
fill and Recommended Pivot Table. Learn how to visualize your data to 
better understand what is really going on with those important numbers 
with the help of Quick Analysis and Chart Formatting Control. This course 
will also help you study and prepare to take the Microsoft's Excel 77-420 
Certification exam.
