The Windows Server 2003 Managing a Network Environment course from LearnKey prepares you with the knowledge and skills needed to install, configure, and administer Microsoft Windows Server 2003. Aaron Spurlock shows you techniques for monitoring and optimizing physical and logical devices. He will guide you through managing users, computers and groups, configuring to resources and disaster recovery contingency planning. At the conclusion of the course, you will understand how to manage and maintain a Windows Server 2003 environment.
