The Interconnecting Cisco Networking Devices: Accelerated v2.0 (CCNAX) course is a fast-paced, hands-on approach to CCNA exam preparation. Students will cover the objectives from both the ICND1 and ICND2 courses and will focus on tackling the objectives for the CCNA comb exam. This accelerated approach requires longer than usual days, with approximately 10 hours of instruction and exercises per day. You will receive the following when you enroll:
<br><br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Access to our online e-learning and video preparation materials introducing you to the basics of networking.
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Free Exam Voucher
<br><br>
The CCNAX combines ICND1, which offers fundamental networking knowledge and ICND2, which will provide an advanced look at troubleshooting and introducing security and wireless topics. Both courses incorporate a large proportion of hands-on lab experience.
