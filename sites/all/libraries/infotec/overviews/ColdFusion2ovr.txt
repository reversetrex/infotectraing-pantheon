With a slew of advanced new features, ColdFusion MX 7 represents both an opportunity and a challenge for those developing Web apps with it. The opportunity, of course, lies in taking their applications to new and increasingly sophisticated levels. The challenge is digging deep enough into ColdFusion to discover the features and techniques that will take them there. The ColdFusion Advanced Topics class makes this information not only accessible but also easily digestible for intermediate and advanced ColdFusion developers.
