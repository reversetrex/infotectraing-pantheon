Students who are working towards becoming career ready must have a working 
knowledge of Word 2013. Computer expert Robin Posnack will teach you the 
basics of the software; from there, she will build on concepts and ideas 
until you have the software figured out. You will be prepared to create 
résumés, cover letters, newsletters and more. The course will also teach 
you how to create and manage documents and format text, paragraphs, and 
sections. Plus, learn how to create tables and lists and insert and format 
objects. This course will prepare you to sit for the Microsoft Word 2013 
Exam 77-418.
