<p>This three-day instructor-led course provides training to Microsoft customers in the effective management of change within an enterprise operations environment. In addition, it provides a knowledge base with respect to changing quadrant for those who deliver Microsoft customer solutions, such as technical account managers (TAMs), consultants, and go-to partners. The core underlying and foundational intellectual property (IP) is the operations guides. The operations guides for change, release, and configuration management provide the detailed foundational information to drive the courseware.</p>
