National studies warn that many of today's teens have failed to develop 
vital emotional abilities they will need to go through life. Some of these 
emotional abilities are handling conflict, planning ahead, resisting 
anxiety and learning to control hot-tempered reactions. Using real life 
scenarios, this program actually teaches students emotional self-control 
by showing them four basic ways to evaluate their own feelings and 
understand when they might be moving in the wrong direction.
