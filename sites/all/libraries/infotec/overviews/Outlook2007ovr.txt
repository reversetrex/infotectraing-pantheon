This course will provide you with the skills you need to start sending and responding to email in Microsoft Outlook 2007, as well as maintaining your Calendar, scheduling meetings, and working with tasks and notes. In this course, you will compose and send email, schedule appointments and meetings, manage contact information and tasks, and use notes.   You will customize your Outlook environment, calendar, mails, folders, and also track, share, assign, and quickly locate various Outlook items.
