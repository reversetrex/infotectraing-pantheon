Number concepts like fractions or percents often act as a barrier for 
students, keeping them from realizing their full potential. This course 
aims to give students a firm foundation in basic mathematic skills to help 
them become adept at operations involving fractions, decimals, and 
percents and to understand the relationship that exists among them. Using 
graphics, demonstrations, and plain language, this course gives clear 
illustrations of the meaning behind the different operations involving 
these numbers.
