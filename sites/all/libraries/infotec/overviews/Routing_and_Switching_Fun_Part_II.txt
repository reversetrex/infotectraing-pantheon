In LearnKey's Routing and Switching Fundamentals 2013 Part II course, users will gain the skills necessary to prepare for and take exam 200-101 ICND2. Users will learn VLSM, IPv6, OSPF and EIGRP protocols as well as learning to use access lists using NAT and DHCP. This course also covers 2800 and 3800 routers using various standard protocols, learn how ACLs are used to protect networks. At the completion of this course users will be prepared for the CCNA Routing and Switching certification.
