What's new in Access 2013? Along with a new look and similar features you 
used in Access 2010, you will be introduced to the new web apps feature. 
In Access 2013 you can design your own or use templates to create apps 
that can be shared with your coworkers or friends. LearnKey expert Jason 
Manibog will take you step-by-step through this course; starting with 
database management and navigation. Users will learn to use the tools 
provided in Access 2013 to design and maintain a basic database.
