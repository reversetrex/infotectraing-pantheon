SharePoint 2010 is Microsoft's newest version of its integrated suite of server capabilities that can help improve organizational effectiveness by providing comprehensive content management and enterprise search, accelerating shared business processes, and facilitating information sharing across boundaries for better business insight. SharePoint 2010 has been completely rebuilt from the ground up to give businesses a single infrastructure for all of their business needs. This course will cover all of the objectives to help you pass Microsoft's 70-667 exam, while maintaining an in-depth training environment that will have students installing, configuring, and administering SharePoint 2010 in no time.
