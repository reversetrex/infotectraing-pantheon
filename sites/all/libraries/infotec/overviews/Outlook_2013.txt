Are you a student or office worker who needs to communicate with others 
about projects, assignments, or events? Or have you just purchased a new 
computer and it came with Office 2013 pre-installed? If you answered yes 
to either of these questions then this is the course for you. With Outlook 
2013 you will learn how to communicate with others on projects, events, 
and assignments. In this course you will learn everything you need to know 
to become proficient and efficient with Outlook. LearnKey expert Michael 
Meskers will teach you tips and tricks that will save you time and energy 
when working in Outlook.
