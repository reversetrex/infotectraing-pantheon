If you are chasing down a career in video and audio production, having a 
working knowledge of Adobe's Premiere Pro CS6 software is key. This course 
will teach you how to effectively use the new Premiere Pro CS6 software, 
allowing you to edit video and audio more effectively and efficiently. 
LearnKey expert Ryan James will teach you how to customize the program, 
helping your workflow fly. He will also teach you how to use the new 
interface and how to manage and view assets in the project panel. Plus, 
learn how to fix video with the Three-Way Color Correction Tool.
