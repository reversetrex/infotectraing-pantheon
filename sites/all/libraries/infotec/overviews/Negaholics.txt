Do phrases like, "It won't work," "Can't be done," or "It'll never happen!" ring in your ears day after day? Then chances are you're dealing with a Negaholic, an individual addicted to negative thinking and behaviors. When negativity spreads unchecked in the workplace, morale plummets, motivation vanishes, productivity slows and everyone is miserable! This course will give you real-world examples that you can use when you encounter a Negaholic. You'll be absolutely amazed as negative attitudes are transformed into positive behaviors right before your eyes!
