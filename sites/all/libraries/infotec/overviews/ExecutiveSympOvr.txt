The 2003 Executive Symposium is targeted to executive management that is involved in overseeing IT projects/teams.The ideal student is an individual who may handle the budgets, the people, the policies, but may never get involved in the actual "doing" of the tasking. It is not necessary or time supportive for this executive to become certified or to know all the intricacies, however, it is imperative that the individual have a <em>birds-eye view</em> understanding.
