Word processing is the use of computers to create, revise, and save documents for printing and future retrieval. The course  will provide student with the basic concepts required to produce basic business documents, customize and automate the way Microsoft Word 2007 works. It also improves the quality of work by enhancing documents with customized Microsoft Word 2007 elements. In this course, student will create complex documents in Microsoft Word 2007 by adding components such as customized lists, tables, charts, and graphics. Student will also create personalized Microsoft Word 2007 efficiency tools.
