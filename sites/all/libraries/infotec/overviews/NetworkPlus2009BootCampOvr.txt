This course will prepare students to take the N10-0004 CompTIA Network+ Certification exam, for the objectives released in January 2009.  CompTIA Network+ Certification has been created as a benchmark for local area networking installation and support. The qualification is endorsed by Microsoft, Novell, Cisco, Compaq, and Lotus, among other leading vendors.  This qualification is particularly recommended for students who wish to progress to the Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) or Microsoft MCSE/MCSA/MCITP qualifications.
