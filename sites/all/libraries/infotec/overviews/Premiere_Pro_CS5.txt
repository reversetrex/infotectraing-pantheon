The Premiere Pro CS5 course is designed to teach individuals everything there is to know about video production, video editing, and how to use Adobe Premiere Pro CS5 to create video communication masterpieces. This course is mapped to Adobe's most recent ACA (Adobe Certified Associate) exam, and upon the completion of this course, you will be prepared to take the Visual Communication using Premiere Pro CS5 ACA test with confidence. Ryan walks you step-by-step through the entire video life cycle beginning with pre-production and planning, and ending with editing it all together in Premiere Pro CS5. If you want to take your videographer skills to the next level and become a video professional, this is the course for you.
