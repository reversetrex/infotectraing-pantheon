In LearnKey's Cisco Routing and Switching Fundamentals course you will learn to plan, configure, and operate simple WAN and switched LAN networks using Cisco products. At the end of this course you will have the skills to successfully install, operate, and troubleshoot a small branch office network. This course will prepare you for Cisco ICND1; exam #640-822 the first exam to Cisco's certification path for Routing and Switching.
