LearnKey's Implementing Unified Wireless Networks course will teach you how to design, install, configure, monitor and perform basic troubleshooting of Cisco WLAN in small and medium-sized business and enterprise installations. This course is designed to help you prepare for Cisco's IUWNE exam, an associate-level certification in the wireless field. Reinforce what you learn with instructor-assisted, hands-on labs that include creating an ad-hoc connection and analyzing the communication, configuring a controller and access point (AP) and much more.
