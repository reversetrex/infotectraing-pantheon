Premiere is Adobe's nonlinear video editing software. With the Adobe 
Premiere course from LearnKey you'll learn how to combine and edit video, 
audio, effects, animation, titles and other elements to build a completed 
video project. Expert instructor Andy Anderson shows you how to get 
started assembling your video and takes you step-by-step through the 
production process all the way to project completion. At the conclusion of 
this course, you will feel comfortable working in the Premiere environment 
to assemble and edit digital video projects.
