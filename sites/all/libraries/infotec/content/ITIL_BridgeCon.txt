<p>The course covers the subject areas of all five (5) lifecycle stages which are new to V3 and those existing subject areas of V2 which have undergone significant change. It bridges the gap between the ITIL V2 Service Manager Certificate in IT Service Management and the V3 Advanced  Certification, the ITIL Expert. Service Design - Service Catalog Management, SLM, Availability Management, IT Service Continuity Management, Supplier Management, Information Security Management, and Capacity Management.</p>
