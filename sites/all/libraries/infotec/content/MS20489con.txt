<p><strong>Module 1: Creating Robust and Efficient Apps for SharePoint</strong></p>
<blockquote>In this module, you will review key aspects of the apps for SharePoint development platform, including capabilities, packaging and infrastructure, client-side programming for SharePoint, and app security. You will also learn about how to optimize the performance of your apps.</blockquote>
<p>Lessons</p>
<ul>
  <li>Apps for SharePoint</li>
  <li>Communicating with SharePoint from an App</li>
  <li>Authenticating and Authorizing Apps for SharePoint</li>
  <li>Designing Apps for Performance</li>
</ul>
