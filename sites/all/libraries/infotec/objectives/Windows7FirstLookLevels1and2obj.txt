<p><strong>Level 1</strong>:</p>

<p>Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Log on to Windows 7, explore its  interface, and identify the different components present in the interface.</li>
  <li>Customize the Windows 7 desktop.</li>
  <li>Manage files and folders.</li>
  <li>Use the common tools and programs  available in Windows 7.</li>
  <li>Browse  the Internet.</li>
</ul>

  <li>Work with applications in Windows 7. </li>
  <li>Work with devices. </li>
  <li>Manage networks. </li>
