<ul>

  <li>Optimize IIS 7 for Performance and Stability.</li>
  <li>Design IIS 7 for Security.</li>

  <li>Design and prepare a Terminal Services infrastructure.</li>
  <li>Design a terminal services maintenance strategy.</li>
