<p>On course completion, students will be able to:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Describe the features of different network protocols and products for LANs, WANs, and wireless networks.</li>
  <li>Understand the functions and features of TCP/IP addressing and protocols.</li>
  <li>Identify threats to network resources and appropriate security countermeasures.</li>
  <li>Install and configure network cabling and appliances.</li>
  <li>Manage, monitor, and troubleshoot networks.</li>
</ol>
