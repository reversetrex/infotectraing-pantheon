<p>After completing this course, students will be able to:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Describe the features and functions of Group Policy</li>
    <li>Use Group Policy management tools to manage security policies</li>
    <li>Design a Group Policy infrastructure</li>
    <li>Describe the Group Policy processing architecture</li>
    <li>Back up, restore, import, and copy Group Policy Objects through the Group Policy Management Console</li>
    <li>Use Windows PowerShell to manage Group Policy</li>
    <li>Implement security using Group Policy</li>
    <li>Configure the desktop environment with Group Policy</li>
    <li>Configure roaming profiles and Folder Redirection</li>
    <li>Assign and publish software packages</li>
    <li>Implement AppLocker an software restriction policies</li>
    <li>Create and deploy Administrative Templates</li>
    <li>Configure Group Policy   preferences</li>
</ul>
