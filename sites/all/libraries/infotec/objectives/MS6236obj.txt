<p>After completing this course, students will be able to:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Describe SQL Server Reporting Services and its components.</li>
  <li>Create a Reporting Services report.</li>
  <li>Enhance a Reporting Services report.</li>
  <li>Create and manipulate data sets.</li>
  <li>Use report models to implement reporting for business users.</li>
  <li>Configure report publishing and execution settings.</li>
  <li>Implement subscriptions for reports.</li>
  <li>Administer Reporting Services.</li>
  <li>SQL Server accounts (users and permissions).</li>
</ul>
