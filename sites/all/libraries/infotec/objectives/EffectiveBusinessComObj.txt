<p><strong>Written Communications</strong></p>

<p>Upon successful completion of this part of the course, students will be able to:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Organize and write clear and concise email and instant messages that are appropriate to your company's business culture.</li>
  <li>Write effective business communications.</li>
  <li>Write letters that are clear, precise, and appropriate to your audience.</li>
  <li>Organize and write a business proposal, complete with visuals, and an executive summary that employ effective strategies of persuasion.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Oral Communications</strong></p>

<p>Upon successful completion of this part of the course, students will be able to:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Listen in an active, engaged manner and take good notes.</li>
  <li>Participate in, organize, and conduct a meeting.</li>
  <li>Organize, write, and deliver a professional presentation.</li>
</ul>
