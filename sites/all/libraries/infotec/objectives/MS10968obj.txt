<p>After completing this course, students will be able to:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Describe the Office 365 FastTrack deployment methodology.</li>
  <li>Identify the benefits of the FastTrack approach compared to the current deployment process.</li>
  <li>Plan the Pilot phase of the FastTrack deployment process.</li>
