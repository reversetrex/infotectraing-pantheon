<p>On course completion, you will be able to:</p>
<ul>
 <li>Identify network attack strategies and defenses.</li>
 <li>Understand the principles of organizational security and the elements of effective security policies.</li>
 <li>Know the technologies and uses of cryptographic standards and products.</li>
 <li>Identify network- and host-based security technologies and practices.</li>
 <li>Describe how wireless and remote access security is enforced.</li>
 <li>Describe the standards and products used to enforce security on web and communications technologies.</li>
 <li>Identify strategies for ensuring business continuity, fault tolerance, and disaster recovery.</li>
</ul>
